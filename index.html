About Us

Started by CU Boulder students to keep their bikes safe, we wanted to help others do the same.

It is reported that 1 in 13 bikes on a university campus gets stolen. We work to reduce that number as much as possible by making it fast and easy to recover it in the case of a successful theft, and by reducing the possibility of it being taken in the first place. The last thought on a university student or faculty's mind shouldn't be worrying if their bike will get stolen from universtiy grounds, so we developed Bike Buddy.

WE BELIEVE IN EASE OF USABILITY

It doesn't matter if you have a great product when it isn't user friendly and easy to use. We work as hard as possible to make using Bike Buddy easy to use for everyone. You are given all the tools you need to keep your bike safe.

Have a problem with Bike Buddy? Let us know about it by heading over to our contact page!
